Weekly ETC Reminder Email

 
@{variables('Username')}, your weekly updates for the engagements where ETCs are overdue or due soon:
 
 

Hi @{variables('Username')},

 

We're reaching out to remind you of the ETC deadlines that are nearing or have been missed for engagements under your management.

 

If there are no recent changes in the financial status of an engagement since your last ETC, and you believe an exemption is justified to exclude this engagement from subsequent follow-ups, please apply for a one using the link at the bottom. We are actively encouraging exemptions, as this will provide valueable insight and prevent you from being chased! If you have any questions please reach out to an FMA.

Common reasons for applications include, but is not limited to:

  • Holding a code open due to pending IOM or a third-party invoice
  • The engagement is an invesment code and you don't need regular ETCs
  • Tax reasons, such as withholding tax or CBTS

 

Please note that submitting ETCs regularly is essential for managing your engagement economics in Mercury, as well as the revenue recognition in firm's performance management reporting. Failure to submit an ETC within deadline is a breach of EY policy and can have a negative impact on decision-making for our engagements and business.

 
Please take the necessary action to submit your ETC in Mercury or apply for an exception.
 

* Important: The information provided is based on data as of last Friday, and won't reflect any ETCs submitted this week. Please disregard this email if you have submitted an ETC recently for all your engagement(s).
